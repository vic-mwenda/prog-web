Zero to One Coding Mentorship Program

This will be a 12 Week apprenticeship program, where you will develop new features, improve existing products, and solve large-scale, highly complex technical problems. You’ll work on software engineering teams building the next generation of products, systems, and services. As an apprentice, you will be taken through project-based training, team collaboration, and soft skills. Apprentices will work with our developers, gaining experience with all software development life cycle aspects, including communication, pairing, testing, continuous integration, presentation, and project management. During your time on Apprenticeship at our Technology Park, Mang’u Campus, you’ll learn what it takes to be a top-notch software developer and consultant. We will provide countless opportunities to broaden your experience on challenging projects. Whatever you work on, you’ll be part of the team, and you will grow your networks.

The Program

Your Responsibilities
  • Working with our experienced software developers to understand client requirements and develop custom software solutions.
  • Learning and implementing new technologies and software development best practices.
  • Participating in code reviews and ensuring the quality of the code.
  • Writing documentation and conducting training sessions for clients.
  • Continuously improving your skills and knowledge in software development
  • Participating in client meetings to understand user requirements and provide solutions
Who are we looking for?
  • Recent college graduates with some programming skills or knowledge– preferred languages are Java, C#, JavaScript, Python, PHP, among others.
  • An understanding of relational and/or non-relational databases
  • The ability to be adaptable and flex your style and thinking to the situation.
  • A collaborative working style, working at your best in dynamic teams.
  • Curiosity, hunger for challenge, and a desire to broaden your understanding of the technology world.
  • The drive to succeed and to help others achieve the same.
  • The ability to work with others and build relationships with your peers and clients.
  • Willingness to navigate ambiguous tasks, find suitable solutions, and seek appropriate advice
Our offer
  • The opportunity to work alongside and learn from our talented Software Developers on real client challenges from the start.
  • The support and coaching to grow your software development skills and experience on real client work, tackling real-world technical and business challenges.
  • The prospect of working across all areas of the software development lifecycle, from strategy to implementation and taking on assignments in different sectors.
  • A broad, rich experience base to build an outstanding Software Development career.
  • The opportunity to carry all the assignments in-person from our state-of-the-art Technology park, Mang’u Campus, with fast internet services.
  • We will provide Software as a Service (SaaS) online marketplace where apprentices can host their software solutions and earn revenue.
